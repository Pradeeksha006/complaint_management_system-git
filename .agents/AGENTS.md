# Chatbot FAQ Rules

The chatbot should answer the following citizen‑oriented questions with concise, relevant information drawn from the website. Do **not** modify any existing application logic or UI.

## FAQs

- **How do I file a complaint?**
  - "To file a complaint, click the **\"Submit Complaint\"** button on the homepage, fill out the form with your details and the issue description, then press **Submit**. You’ll receive a confirmation email with your complaint ID."

- **How do I track my complaint?**
  - "To track a complaint, go to the **\"My Complaints\"** page, enter the complaint ID you received in the confirmation email, and click **Track**. The page will show the current status and any updates."

- **Which department handles water complaints?**
  - "Water‑related issues are handled by the **Water Department**. Any complaint that mentions water, leakage, or supply problems will be routed to that department automatically."

- **Where can I submit a complaint?**
  - "You can submit a complaint from the homepage by clicking the **\"Submit Complaint\"** button, or directly via the **/complaint** URL."

- **What details are required to register a complaint?**
  - "Required fields are your full name, email address, selected department, a brief title, and a detailed description of the issue."

- **Can I attach images or documents?**
  - "Yes. The complaint form includes an optional **Attach Files** field where you can upload photos, PDFs, or other supporting documents (max 5 MB per file)."

- **What types of complaints can I raise?**
  - "You can raise water‑related issues, municipal services concerns (sanitation, roads, waste), public service matters (licensing, permits), or any general civic issue."

- **Which department should I choose?**
  - "Select the department that best matches your issue—for water problems choose **Water Department**, for sanitation or road matters choose **Municipal Department**, otherwise select **General**."

- **Can I submit an anonymous complaint?**
  - "The system requires an email for confirmation, so fully anonymous submissions are not supported, but you may omit your name and phone number."

- **What is the complaint registration process?**
  - "1. Submit the complaint via the form.<br>2. Receive a confirmation email with a unique ID.<br>3. The system routes the complaint to the appropriate department.<br>4. Track progress on the **My Complaints** page using the ID."

These rules should be applied whenever the chatbot detects one of the above questions, ensuring the response is relevant and helpful without altering any other functionality.
